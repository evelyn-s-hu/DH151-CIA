Anti Hate Crime Motivation

About the Map

Hate crimes were sorted into five motivation categories - Anti Race, Anti Gender, Anti Ableism, Anti Religion and Anti Sexual Orientation. Anti Race cases are red, Anti Gender cases are green, Anti Sexual Orientation cases are blue, Anti Abelism cases are pink and Anti Religion cases are purple.

Key Finding 1: Motivations aren't equally distributed across hate crimes

Across the United States, there are a significantly less number of Anti Gender motivated hate crimes as there are Anti Race motivated hate crimes.

Key Finding 2: Motivations aren't equally distributed across cities

Across the United States, a city like Seattle will have a greater density of Anti Religion motivated hate crimes compared to other motivated hate crimes.

Specific Motivation Key Findings

Anti Race Motivated Hate Crimes are the most commonly motivated hate crimes across the United States. There is a higher density of cases on the East Coast in comparison to Central United States and the West Coast.

Sources

We used two sources: the United States Hate Crimes (1991-2018) Dataset detailed all the hate crimes from 1991-2018 which we then sorted and assigned longitude and latitude to each city using US Cities Database
